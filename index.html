Our Story

We are Tuuli and Katja, 23 and 25-year-old sisters, athletes, and students from Espoo. Together we are Metsäkoivubeach, an energetic and colorful beach volley team.

Beachvolley

Modern, constantly growing and appealing to everyone. Beach volley has been an Olympic sport since 1996 and is a crowd favorite everywhere in the world. The sport is constantly gaining foothold in Finland.

FAQ

Yes, beach volleyball is different from normal volleyball ;) It's played as 2 vs 2 games (as opposed to 6 vs 6) and it has some restrictions for how you can touch the ball. Of course all of this means that the tactiques and mental game is also really different.

How it began

Our mom started a volleyball club in our primary school, so at 7 and 9 years old we started playing volleyball. First for fun but it soon became a lifestyle and we ended playing as semi-professionals in Finnish League team LP-Vampula.

4 years ago we started playing beach volley for fun during the summer. The love for the sport sparked immediately and grew steadily until we realized we'd started to enjoy beach volley more than regular volleyball.

Where are we now

Every year our game has gone forward a lot. In 2017 we started playing the Finnish Tour with not that much success. In 2018 we finished in the top 3 in 3/4 tournaments and made our way into the Finnish Championship finals where we finished at 7th place.

Last summer we had two 3rd places and two 2nd places on the Finnish Tour and finished 4th in the Finnish Championship finals. We also played our first international tournaments. In June we played in a Northern Europe Zonal Event (NEVZA) in Norway. As the final tournament of the summer we travelled to China and competed in our first World Tour qualification.

What's next

This year we are giving up volleyball and concentrating 100% to beach volley. The autumn will be dedicated to strong physical practice and a lot of technique. In the spring we will travel to Brazil to get a head start on playing outdoors.

Next summer we aim to concentrate on international tournaments; we aim to play some tournaments in Asia in April and then play in Europe and on the Finnish Tour.

Our goals

Next summer our goal is the Finnish Championship and getting into the main draw in World Tour events.

Our dream is to represent Finland in the European and World Championships. We are dedicated to working hard in order to reach our goals!
